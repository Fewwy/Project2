Journey of an arranger

Hello, my name Paul. I love math and music, but who cares about math, right? So, I'll tell you about my path in music I finished piano music school in 2007, and after that I sorta abandoned performing. I tried singing, and other instruments, like flute or guitar, but nothing made me passionate enough to start practicing music again.

Everything changed near summer 2012. I was math instructor in summer camp for kids from high school. Our manager asked me to direct rehearsals for musical performance at the end of this math camp. We decided that using backing tracks are boring, but we did not have sheet music for our instrumentalists to play. So I arranged it by ear myself. Two years later, with help of my fellow students, we arranged almost entire rockopera "Jesus Christ Superstar"

although I have to warn you that almost all musicians are kids, so if you have sensetive ear, they will probably bleed. A lot.

If you are interested in sheet music that I made, you should check my page at musescore.com. I upload any sheet music that is polished enough here. You can check it online, it will look smth like this Bound By One Chain by Павел Томас or download it if you have paid for pro subscription. Also you can download it for free via some shady usage of their internal API, that I TOTALLY CAN'T RECOMMEND OR APPROVE. Or you can write me an email and I'll send everything you'd want to you. Also, in an unimaginable case you liked my arrangements and you want me to make one for you, you can write me an about it too.
